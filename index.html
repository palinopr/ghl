Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the rental?

All rentals include fuel, licensed captain, safety equipment, ice, water, and basic sound system. Additional amenities vary by boat.

Can I bring my own food and drinks?

Yes! You're welcome to bring your own food and beverages. We also offer catering options if you prefer.

What happens if weather is bad?

Safety first! We offer free rescheduling or full refund if weather conditions are unsafe for boating.

Do I need a boating license?

No! All our boats come with experienced, licensed captains who handle all the navigation.
